# Week 3 Sprint Retrospective

This retrospective is public and sanitized. It focuses on team process, delivery, and technical coordination without personal or private conflict details.

- The team successfully narrowed MVP v1 scope after customer review and kept the focus on core functionality: registration, authorization, vehicle registration/display, and basic chat.
- GitHub issues, Sprint planning, and linked PBIs improved visibility of current work and made it easier to track implementation against [Sprint 1 - MVP v1 Foundation](https://github.com/LAMBA-23/LAMBA/milestone/1).
- The team delivered meaningful integration progress across Android, backend, and documentation, which strengthened the MVP foundation for the next Sprint.

## What did not go well

## Action points
